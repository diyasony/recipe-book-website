Vegetable Fried Rice

← Go to Homepage

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/4 cup spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Optional: chili sauce, garlic

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic (if using) for 30 seconds.
  2. Add all the chopped vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 3–4 mins.
  3. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss everything on high heat for 2 mins until well mixed and hot.
  5. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot!

Nutritional Information (Approximate per serving)

Calories: 400 | Carbs: 55g | Protein: 9g | Fat: 13g | Fiber: 4g
