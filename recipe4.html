Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (or grated cheese)
  • 1 tbsp butter (softened)

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of bread.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and grill the sandwich on medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Press gently while grilling for a crisp crust and melted inside.
  5. Cut and serve hot with ketchup or soup!

Nutritional Information (Approximate per serving)

Calories: 380 | Carbs: 30g | Protein: 11g | Fat: 22g | Fiber: 2g
